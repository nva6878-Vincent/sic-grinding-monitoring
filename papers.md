# Key Papers & References

A curated reading list for adaptive control of SiC precision grinding.

## Primary inspiration

- **Guo, B., Zhang, Q., Peng, Q., Zhuang, J., Wu, F., & Zhang, Q. (2019).**
  Silicon Carbide Surface Quality Prediction Based on Artificial
  Intelligence Methods on Multi-sensor Fusion Detection Test Platform.
  *Machining Science and Technology*, 23(1).
  DOI: [10.1080/10910344.2018.1486414](https://doi.org/10.1080/10910344.2018.1486414)
  
  Combines multi-sensor fusion, Lempel-Ziv complexity for stability
  analysis, and a KPCA + Modified ELM predictive model. Uses genetic
  algorithm optimization to stabilize ELM weights. Reports max prediction
  error of 4.8% on surface roughness.

## Foundational techniques

- **Lempel, A., & Ziv, J. (1976).** On the Complexity of Finite
  Sequences. *IEEE Transactions on Information Theory*, 22(1), 75–81.
  — Original LZ76 complexity definition.

- **Kaspar, F., & Schuster, H. G. (1987).** Easily calculable measure
  for the complexity of spatiotemporal patterns. *Physical Review A*,
  36(2), 842.
  — Practical algorithm for computing LZC on finite sequences.
